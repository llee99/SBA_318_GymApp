Body Building

  • Hamstring Curls

    Machine based hamstring hypertrophy exercise

    Equipment: Leg Curl Machine

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Load Weight, Position back of heels under the heel rest, Pull bar towards back slowly and squeeze

  • Cable Flys

    Machine based chest hypertrophy exercise

    Equipment: Cable Machine, Grip Attachments

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Load Weight, Grip Attachments wider than shoulder length apart, Pull inwards towards chest, Slow squeeze for maximum growth

  • Lat Pulldown

    Machine based upper back hypertrophy exercise

    Equipment: Elevated cable station with seat, Pulldown bar attachment

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Load Weight, Grip bar wider than shoulder length apart, Pull down towards chest slowly and squeeze
