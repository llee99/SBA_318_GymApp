Calisthenics

  • Air Squat

    Classic bodyweight leg and lower back exercise

    Equipment: None! (Optional added weight)

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Brace abs and keep lower back straight, Squat downwards until thighs are at least parallel to the ground, push back upwards to start position

  • Push Up

    Classic bodyweight chest exercise

    Equipment: None! (Optional added weight)

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Get into prone front leaning rest, lower chest to 1 inch above ground then press upwards back to start position

  • Pull Up

    Classic bodyweight total body lift

    Equipment: Pull up bar (Optional added weight)

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Grip bar at least shoulder length apart, Pull upwards until upper chest is even with bar, then lower body back to start position
