Power Lifting

  • Squat

    Compound leg and lower back lift

    Equipment: Straight Barbell, Squat Rack, Weights (optional)

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Load Weight (optional), Position traps under the bar, Un-rack the bar, take 3 steps back (medium, large, small), Squat

  • Bench Press

    Compound chest lift

    Equipment: Straight Barbell, Bench Rack, Weights (optional)

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Load Weight (optional), Grip bar shoulder length apart, Un-rack the bar, Bench

  • Dead Lift

    Compound total body lift

    Equipment: Straight Barbell, Platform (optional), Weights (optional)

    Instructions: Stretch, Warm-Up, Load Weight (optional), Squat into pocket, Grip bar shoulder length apart, Pull
